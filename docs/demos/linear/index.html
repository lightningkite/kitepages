Features

A new species of product tool. Purpose-built for modern teams with AI workflows at its core, Linear sets a new standard for planning and building products.

Built for Speed

Every interaction in Linear is optimized for speed. Keyboard-first design, instant search, and sub-50ms response times across the board.

AI-Native Workflows

From drafting PRDs to pushing PRs, AI agents work alongside your team. Delegate tasks or collaborate on complex issues end-to-end.

Opinionated by Design

Linear is shaped by the practices and principles of the best product teams. Less configuration, more momentum.

Make product operations self-driving

Turn conversations and customer feedback into actionable issues that are routed, labeled, and prioritized for the right team. Triage becomes effortless when the system understands context.

Automated Intake

Issues created from Slack, email, and support tools arrive labeled and prioritized. No manual sorting required.

Smart Routing

Work is automatically assigned to the right team based on content, history, and current workload.

Priority Intelligence

Linear learns from your team’s behavior to surface what matters most, reducing noise and restoring focus.

Define the product direction

Plan and navigate from idea to launch. Align your team with product initiatives, strategic roadmaps, and clear, up-to-date project documents.

Initiatives

Group projects into strategic themes. Track progress toward company goals with a single view.

Roadmaps

Visual timelines that stay in sync with your work. No more stale spreadsheets or forgotten slide decks.

Project Documents

Rich documents that live alongside your issues. Write PRDs, specs, and briefs where the work happens.

Move work forward across teams and agents

Build and deploy AI agents that work alongside your team. Work on complex tasks together or delegate entire issues end-to-end.

Human + Agent Collaboration

Agents draft code, write tests, and propose PRs. Humans review, discuss, and merge. Every action is traceable and auditable.

Full Autonomy When You Need It

Assign an issue to an agent and it handles the rest. From understanding the problem to shipping the fix.

Review PRs and agent output

Understand code changes at a glance with structural diffs for human and agent output. Review, discuss, and merge within Linear.

  • Structural diff view for easier code review
  • Inline comments and discussion threads
  • Agent-generated summaries of changes
  • One-click merge with CI/CD integration
  • Multi-repo PR views (coming soon)

Understand progress at scale

Take the guesswork out of product development with project updates, analytics, and dashboards that surface what needs your attention.
