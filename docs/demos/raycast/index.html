Features

One interface, everything you need. Raycast replaces dozens of apps and context switches with a single keyboard shortcut.

🔍 App Launcher

Launch applications instantly. Type a few characters and hit enter. No more hunting through folders or the dock.

📄 File Search

Find any file on your Mac in milliseconds. Search by name, content, or type. Preview files without opening them.

⚙️ System Commands

Control your Mac without touching the mouse. Toggle Bluetooth, eject drives, empty trash, lock screen, and more.

📅 Schedule

See your upcoming meetings at a glance. Join video calls with a single keystroke. Never miss a standup again.

🔗 Clipboard History

Never lose copied text again. Search through your clipboard history, pin frequent items, and paste with a shortcut.

✏️ Snippets

Stop typing the same things. Create text snippets with dynamic placeholders for dates, cursors, and clipboard content.

Don’t repeat yourself

Automate the things you do all the time. Raycast gives you three powerful tools to eliminate repetitive work.

Snippets

Tired of typing the same thing? Create a snippet and insert it by typing its keyword.

;addr → 3rd Floor, 1 Ashley Road
        WA14 2DT Altrincham, Cheshire

;sig  → Best regards,
        Alex Thompson
        alex@company.com

Quicklinks

Say goodbye to open tabs. Create quicklinks to launch anything from anywhere.

gh {query} → github.com/search?q={query}
jira {id}  → company.atlassian.net/browse/{id}
docs {q}   → docs.google.com/search?q={q}

Hotkeys & Aliases

Speed up your workflow by assigning hotkeys or aliases to common commands.

  • ⌥ Space — Clipboard History
  • ⌘ ⇧ M — Toggle Microphone
  • ⌘ ⇧ D — Toggle Do Not Disturb
