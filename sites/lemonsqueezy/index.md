## Frequently Asked Questions

>| What is a Merchant of Record?
  A Merchant of Record (MoR) is the legal entity that sells goods to end customers. As your MoR, Lemon Squeezy handles payment processing, tax compliance, chargebacks, and fraud prevention on your behalf. You focus on building your product.

>| How does tax compliance work?
  We automatically calculate, collect, and remit sales tax, VAT, and GST in 100+ countries. You don't need to register for tax IDs, file returns, or worry about changing regulations. It's all handled.

>| Can I migrate from Stripe or Paddle?
  Yes. We offer assisted migration with dedicated support. Most teams are fully migrated within a week. We'll help you move customers, subscriptions, and historical data.

>| What payment methods do you support?
  Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and local payment methods in supported regions.

>| Is there a free plan?
  Yes. Lemon Squeezy is free to start. No monthly fees. You only pay a small percentage per transaction. Volume discounts available for larger businesses.
