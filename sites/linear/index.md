## Make product operations self-driving

Turn conversations and customer feedback into actionable issues that are routed, labeled, and prioritized for the right team. Triage becomes effortless when the system understands context.

|||
### Automated Intake

Issues created from Slack, email, and support tools arrive labeled and prioritized. No manual sorting required.
---
### Smart Routing

Work is automatically assigned to the right team based on content, history, and current workload.
---
### Priority Intelligence

Linear learns from your team's behavior to surface what matters most, reducing noise and restoring focus.

Plan and navigate from idea to launch. Align your team with product initiatives, strategic roadmaps, and clear, up-to-date project documents.
