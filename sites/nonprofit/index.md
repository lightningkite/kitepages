## Current Campaigns

|||

::: card
### Eagle Creek Corridor
Goal: $2.4M
A 640-acre parcel connecting two existing preserves, creating an unbroken wildlife corridor along Eagle Creek. Development pressure is mounting — we need to act by summer 2026.
:::

---

::: card
### Wetland Restoration at Marsh Lake
Goal: $800K
Restoring 120 acres of degraded wetland to filter water, reduce flooding, and provide critical habitat for migrating waterfowl.
:::

---

::: card
### Youth Conservation Corps
Goal: $150K
Funding summer internships for 40 local high school students to learn land stewardship, ecological monitoring, and conservation careers.
:::
