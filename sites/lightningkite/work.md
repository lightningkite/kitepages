# Our Work

Over 300 projects delivered across mobile, web, and IoT. Here are some highlights.

## Blackstone Products

![Blackstone Griddle](blackstone-griddle.webp =x400){showcase}

Blackstone came to us with a critical challenge: their IoT-connected smart grill platform needed a complete overhaul. We rebuilt the cross-platform mobile app from the ground up, implemented OTA firmware update infrastructure, and built a robust cloud backend to manage thousands of connected devices.

|||
::: card
### Mobile App
Cross-platform native app for iOS and Android with real-time grill monitoring, temperature controls, and recipe integration.
:::
---
::: card
### Firmware Updates
Over-the-air update system that reliably delivers firmware to devices in the field without user intervention.
:::
---
::: card
### Cloud Backend
Scalable IoT backend handling device telemetry, user accounts, and real-time communication between app and grill.
:::
|||
