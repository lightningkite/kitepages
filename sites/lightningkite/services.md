## Frequently Asked Questions

>| How long does a typical project take?
   It depends on scope, but most projects range from 3 to 9 months. We'll give you a realistic timeline during discovery -- no inflated estimates, no surprises.

>| What's your pricing model?
   We work on a time-and-materials basis with weekly invoicing. You see exactly what you're paying for. We can also scope fixed-price milestones for well-defined features.

>| Do you work with startups?
   Absolutely. We've helped startups go from napkin sketch to App Store. We're also experienced with enterprise clients who need to modernize legacy systems.

>| Can you take over an existing codebase?
   Yes. We regularly inherit projects from other teams. We'll do a thorough code audit first so we understand what we're working with before making changes.

>| What happens after launch?
   We offer ongoing support and maintenance. Most of our clients stay with us long-term -- our longest partnership is over 5 years and counting.
