## Front Range Logistics -- Warehouse Rewire

::: bg https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1610463076431-2717271d692d?w=1400&q=80
:::

Complete electrical rewire of a 40,000 sq ft distribution warehouse in Commerce City. The existing knob-and-tube wiring (yes, in a warehouse) was a serious fire hazard. We replaced the entire system -- new 600A three-phase service, sub-panels throughout, LED high-bay lighting, and motor circuits for conveyor systems.

|||
### Scope
- 600A three-phase service upgrade
- 12 sub-panel installations
- 200+ LED high-bay fixtures
- Motor control circuits for conveyor lines
- Emergency lighting and exit signs
---
### Results
- Zero days of operational downtime
- 55% reduction in lighting energy costs
- Passed all inspections on first walk-through
- Completed in 6 weeks (2 weeks ahead of schedule)
|||

Restaurant kitchens are some of the most demanding commercial electrical environments -- high-amperage circuits for ovens and ranges, GFCI protection near water, dedicated circuits for walk-in coolers, and aesthetic lighting for the dining room that sets the right mood.

- Dedicated circuits for all kitchen equipment
- Walk-in cooler and freezer wiring
- Dining room dimming and accent lighting
- Exterior patio string lighting
- POS system and security pre-wire
---
### Results
- Opened on time for planned grand opening
- Passed health department and building inspection same week
- Owner called us back for emergency panel repair -- on-site in under an hour

## Ridgeline Medical Group -- Dental Office Buildout

Specialized electrical for a six-operatory dental practice in Highlands Ranch. Dental offices have unique requirements -- dedicated circuits for each chair's equipment, specialized lighting, x-ray machine power, and clean grounding for sensitive electronics.

|||

- Emergency generator connection
---

## Peak Fitness -- Gym LED Retrofit

![Warehouse interior](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1555983076-1d4ce0cec1aa?w=1400&q=80)

LED lighting retrofit for a 15,000 sq ft fitness center in Arvada. Replaced 96 fluorescent fixtures with high-efficiency LED panels and added occupancy-based controls to utility areas. The project paid for itself in energy savings within 18 months.

|||
### Scope
- 96 fixture LED retrofit (4-foot and 8-foot)
- Occupancy sensors in restrooms and storage
- Daylight harvesting in front cardio area
- New lighting contactors and controls
- Utility rebate application assistance
---
### Results
- 58% reduction in lighting energy costs
- $14,000 utility rebate captured
- 18-month payback period
- Completed over two weekends with no closure
|||
