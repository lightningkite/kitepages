## Frequently Asked Questions

>| Do you handle permitting?
   Yes. We pull all necessary electrical permits and coordinate inspections with the local AHJ (Authority Having Jurisdiction). Permit costs are included in our proposals.

>| What areas do you serve?
   We serve the entire Denver metro area and Front Range, including Aurora, Lakewood, Arvada, Westminster, Centennial, Littleton, Parker, Castle Rock, and surrounding communities. For projects within 50 miles of Denver, there's no travel charge.

>| Are you licensed and insured?
   Absolutely. We hold a Colorado Master Electrician license (EC.0042817), carry $2M in general liability insurance, and maintain full workers' compensation coverage. We're happy to provide certificates of insurance for your records.

>| Can you work nights and weekends to avoid disrupting our business?
   Yes, and we do it regularly. Many of our retail and restaurant clients need work done outside business hours. We'll schedule around your operations at no additional charge for standard after-hours work.

>| Do you offer maintenance contracts?
   We offer annual and semi-annual preventive maintenance programs for commercial clients. Regular maintenance catches small problems before they become expensive emergencies. Ask us for details.
