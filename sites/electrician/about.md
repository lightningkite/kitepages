## Our Story

Apex Electric started in 2006 with one truck, one master electrician, and a simple idea: commercial electrical work doesn't have to be a headache for the customer.

Twenty years later, we've completed over 500 commercial projects across the Denver metro area. We're still not the biggest contractor in town, and we plan to keep it that way. Every client works directly with the owner. Every project is staffed with licensed journeymen and masters -- never temps, never day labor.

We've built our reputation on three things: showing up when we say we will, doing the job right the first time, and charging a fair price for honest work.
