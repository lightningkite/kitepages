## Our Story

We met at a coffee shop in San Francisco in 2019. James spilled his latte on Emma's laptop, offered to pay for repairs, and somehow turned a disaster into a first date. Four years, two apartments, one rescue dog (hi, Biscuit!), and a million adventures later, James proposed on a foggy morning at Lands End.

We can't wait to celebrate with the people we love most.
